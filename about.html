Who We Are?

Welcome to our e-waste management initiative, where sustainability meets innovation. At trash to treasure, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the disposal and recycling of electronic waste. With a commitment to environmental stewardship, we harness cutting-edge technologies to responsibly process and repurpose electronic devices. Our mission is to minimize the environmental impact of e-waste while maximizing the potential for reuse and recycling. Through education, awareness, and community engagement, we strive to create a greener, cleaner future for generations to come. Join us in our journey towards a more sustainable tomorrow.



Trash to Treasure eWaste is a sustainable and cost-effective electronic waste management company in India. We provide comprehensive e-Recycling services in India for disposal of eWaste efficiently and in an environmentally friendly way, making our facility one of the most sustainable in the country with zero waste discharge technology. Our team is committed to providing you with reliable, affordable, and sustainable solutions for managing your EEE waste.



Join us on our journey to create a more beautiful, sustainable, and green environment. Recycling one million laptops saves the energy equivalent to the electricity used by more than 3,500 US homes in a year. We help in responsive disposal of eWaste.

Think globally, act locally. Every small action towards recycling e-waste contributes to a healthier planet.

Our Virtues

"Reduce, reuse, recycle, reimagine: the path to a greener world starts with responsible e-waste management."

Customer First

At Trash to Treasure eWaste, we prioritize customer needs, offering recycling and sustainable waste management solutions while fulfilling social responsibilities to local communities. As pioneers in e-waste recycling, we're dedicated to impactful projects for a greener future.

Ownership

We hold the belief that ownership comes with the responsibility of managing the growing electronic waste dilemma in India. That's why we offer our clients a hassle-free doorstep collection service for their e-waste, ensuring convenient disposal and a commitment to positive change.

Education

We believe that education is important for sustainability. We aim to provide information on our products and their uses to help you conserve the environment and recycle sustainable electronics and eco-friendly electronics through collaboration with ethical electronic recycling companies.

Responsible

We're deeply passionate about recycling for its power to cut carbon emissions, save energy, and protect against hazardous chemicals like mercury, lead, and cadmium. Our commitment extends to efficiently collecting electronic waste to minimize its impact on our planet.

Respect for people

We're a socially responsible company, prioritizing respect for employees, partners, and customers. Our focus on e-waste management and promoting social responsibility among stakeholders drives our pursuit of sustainable solutions.

Passion to Deliver

Our objective is to provide services and products that prioritize both security and sustainability. Each sustainable e-waste management product undergoes thorough inspection to ensure it meets customer needs and complies with all relevant regulations, thereby guaranteeing sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

To revolutionize e-waste management by offering innovative, sustainable solutions that prioritize environmental stewardship, community engagement, and technological advancement. Our mission is to minimize the environmental impact of electronic waste through responsible recycling practices, education, and advocacy, paving the way for a greener and cleaner future.

Our Vision

To lead globally in e-waste management, setting sustainability, efficiency, and social responsibility benchmarks. Envisioning a world where e-waste is transformed into valuable resources through technology and partnerships. Promoting circular economy principles, fostering environmental consciousness, we aim for a healthier planet for generations.
