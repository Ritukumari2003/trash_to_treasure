Who We Are

Trash to treasure eWaste Management Ltd. is one of the leading companies of India that recycle electrical and electronic (EEE) waste dedicated to e-waste management. Successfully operating since 2014, trash to treasure eWaste Management offers comprehensive, complete, and responsible recycling services.


Our integrated and best-in-class technology ensures environmental safety and sustainability while recycling eWaste. As an electronic recycling company, we are dedicated to minimizing environmental impact and promoting a greener future. With our experience in managing large volumes of high-quality EEE waste, we are able to extract all of the components of an electrical item including metals like mercury, lead, cadmium, PCBs, etc., without leaving a trace behind.


An effective e-waste management system involves several key components. First, collection and transportation include establishing collection points or drop-off centers and providing pick-up services for households and businesses. Second, sorting and categorization separate hazardous materials from reusable or recyclable components, often using both manual and automated processes. Third, recycling and material recovery disassemble devices to retrieve valuable materials like gold, silver, copper, and rare earth metals through techniques such as shredding, smelting, and chemical extraction. Fourth, the treatment of hazardous waste involves safe disposal methods and chemical processes to neutralize harmful components. Lastly, promoting reuse and refurbishment of electronic devices extends their lifespan and reduces the overall volume of e-waste.

Our Services

Electrical & Electronics Recycling


Electrical and electronic recycling, often termed e-recycling, plays a crucial role in environmental sustainability. This process involves disassembling, sorting, and reclaiming materials from discarded electronic devices. Through recycling, valuable resources like metals, plastics, and rare earth elements are recovered, reducing the need for new raw materials and minimizing waste.
Additionally, e-recycling prevents harmful substances like lead and mercury from contaminating the environment. By promoting responsible disposal practices and resource conservation, electrical and electronic recycling contributes significantly to the circular economy and mitigates the impact of electronic waste on ecosystems and human health.

Data Destruction


Data destruction is the deliberate process of eliminating sensitive or unwanted information stored in digital formats. It involves wiping, shredding, or otherwise rendering data irretrievable to prevent unauthorized access.
This practice is crucial for safeguarding privacy, ensuring compliance with data protection regulations, and mitigating security risks when disposing of electronic devices or retiring outdated systems. Effective data destruction methods ensure that information cannot be recovered, maintaining the confidentiality and integrity of sensitive data throughout its lifecycle.

IT and Electronic assets disposition(ITAD)

IT and electronic assets disposition refers to the process of properly managing and disposing of outdated or unwanted technological equipment. This includes devices such as computers, servers, and peripherals.
Effective disposition involves methods like recycling, refurbishing, or securely destroying data to prevent sensitive information from being compromised. With the rapid pace of technological advancement, proper management of electronic assets is crucial for both environmental sustainability and data security, ensuring responsible handling throughout their lifecycle.
